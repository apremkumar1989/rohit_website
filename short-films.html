Short Films

REWIRE

After repeated failure attempts, a situation arises when two men have difference in opinion. One insists in continuing to complete the task whereas the other wants to start over. The story is about how this conflict is handled and who emerges successful.

KILI POLA ( LIKE A PARROT)

Kili Pola (Like a Parrot ) is a stop-motion animation film made as part of an academic course at National Institute of Design (NID), India. This is my first short film.
SYNOPSIS - The story is about the emotional trauma a butcher's son undergoes during his first kill and the consequences.

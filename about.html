About Me

I'm A Rohit, 27 year old Designer based in Bangalore. I finished my Bachelors in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Surathakal ( NITK ) and my Masters in design from National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad ( NID) specialising in Animation Film Design.

The introvert me observes the world around me and inspires my storytelling. With a Mix of logical and creative mind, my design is what I think, see, feel, hear and smell.

P.s - Love Football, Chess and am quite good at it. Also a hardcore nature lover.
